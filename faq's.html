FAQ's

  1. What is Historic Pathways?
    Historic Pathways is a heritage-focused website dedicated to exploring historical sites, monuments, and cultural destinations
  2. Are the destinations featured only from India?
    While our primary focus is on India's rich historical heritage, we also highlight significant global landmarks and ancient sites from around the world.
  3. Can I contribute articles or photos to the website?
    Yes! We welcome contributions from history enthusiasts, travelers, and photographers. Please reach out via our Contact Us page for submission guidelines.
  4. Is the historical information on the website verified?
    We strive to ensure all content is based on credible sources and research.
  5. Do you provide travel booking or guided tours?
    Currently, Historic Pathways does not offer bookings or tours. Our goal is to inform and inspire, but we may collaborate with travel partners in the future.
